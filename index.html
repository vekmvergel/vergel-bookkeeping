About Me

Enforcing Order in Business
Financial Books

My name is Vergel Emmanuel K. Mata, and I am a QuickBooks ProAdvisor and a certified, trained bookkeeper who helps business owners organize, clean up, reconcile, and maintain accurate financial records. I specialize in QuickBooks Online for service-based businesses, product-based businesses, and rental property or property management accounting. My bookkeeping support includes catch-up bookkeeping, transaction categorization, invoice and payment tracking, account reconciliation, expense management, and financial reporting. I provide clear and accurate reports and financial summaries to help business owners understand the financial health and progress of their business.

I maximize the use of cloud accounting systems and AI to help businesses simplify their financial processes and improve efficiency. My focus is to provide reliable bookkeeping support that helps business owners stay organized, make informed financial decisions, and gain more time to focus on business growth. I continue learning new accounting technologies and automation systems to provide accurate and efficient bookkeeping solutions that adapt to the changing needs of modern businesses.
